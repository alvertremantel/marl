## In-Session Notes

- Name selected from shortlist: MARL won over VARVE and OCHRE. Underlying acronym (Microbial Automaton for Reaction-diffusion and Lineage) was the deciding factor.
- Architecture framing shifted significantly during specification: original GoL-derived neighbor-counting frame rejected in favor of reaction-diffusion as primary physics layer. This is the correct frame for the biological regime being modeled.
- 500 × 500 × 200 grid confirmed as target. ~50M voxels. 8 chemical species → ~0.8GB VRAM at float16. 4060 8GB is sufficient.
- Vertical light attenuation identified as the key emergent-structure mechanism. Beer-Lambert column integral, absorber-weighted. Will produce Winogradsky column dynamics without explicit programming — strong paper result.
- Ruleset representation left as open decision (ADR-003). Parametric threshold functions recommended for prototype; program-based representation deferred pending expressivity evaluation.
- HGT propensity as an evolvable parameter confirmed. Second-order dynamics (evolution acting on evolvability) is a publishable angle.
- Direct cell-cell contact deliberately excluded from base architecture. Contact is emergent only.
